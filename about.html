StreetHeat

Version 1.0.0

StreetHeat was built to solve a simple problem:
Finding street food shouldn't be hard.

Traditional maps are often outdated. They send you to empty street corners where a truck "used to be."

We built StreetHeat to be the real-time pulse of the street food scene. By connecting hungry explorers with local vendors, we ensure you never drive to a closed spot again.
